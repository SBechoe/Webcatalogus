Disney Interactive

Disney Interactive is the digital entertainment division of The Walt Disney Company, responsible for producing and distributing interactive media, including video games, mobile apps, and websites. The division was formed in 2008 and has since released numerous successful games, such as the Disney Infinity series, the Kingdom Hearts series, and the Star Wars: Commander mobile game. Disney Interactive's games are known for their engaging storytelling, vibrant visuals, and memorable characters from popular Disney franchises. With a focus on family-friendly entertainment, Disney Interactive has become a major player in the gaming industry, providing a fun and immersive gaming experience for all ages.

Konami games

Konami is a Japanese video game company that was founded in 1969. The company has developed and published numerous iconic games, including the Castlevania series, the Metal Gear Solid series, the Silent Hill series, and Pro Evolution Soccer. Konami's games are known for their innovative gameplay mechanics, immersive storytelling, and high-quality graphics. The company also produces arcade games, trading card games, and other entertainment products. With a strong reputation for excellence in game development, Konami has become a major player in the gaming industry, consistently pushing the boundaries of gaming technology and innovation.

Niantic

Niantic is an American software development company that specializes in creating augmented reality (AR) games and technology. The company was founded in 2010 as an internal startup within Google, and became an independent entity in 2015. Niantic is best known for developing the popular AR game, Pokémon Go, which allows players to catch virtual Pokémon in the real world using their smartphones. The company's other notable projects include Ingress and Harry Potter: Wizards Unite. Niantic's games and technology are known for their immersive and interactive experiences, combining the real world with digital elements. With a focus on using technology to bring people together and encourage exploration, Niantic has become a leader in the field of AR gaming and technology.
